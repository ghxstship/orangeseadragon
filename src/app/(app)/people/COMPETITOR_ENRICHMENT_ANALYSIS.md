# ATLVS People Module: Competitor Enrichment Analysis

**Module**: People (Workforce Management)  
**Analysis Date**: February 2026  
**Version**: 1.0

---

## Executive Summary

The ATLVS People module provides foundational workforce management capabilities including staff rosters, scheduling, recruitment, performance reviews, training, and global travel tracking. Current implementation features innovative "Spatial UI" design patterns with holographic directory cards and life stream profiles.

**Competitive Position**: ATLVS is at **parity** with mid-market solutions for core HR functions but has **significant gaps** in advanced automation, AI-powered insights, employee self-service, and compliance management compared to market leaders.

**Key Opportunities**:
1. AI-powered workforce intelligence and automation
2. Enhanced employee self-service portal
3. Advanced compliance and certification management
4. Real-time collaboration and communication tools
5. Mobile-first experience optimization

### 4.1 Onboarding Flows
| Best Practice | Implementation |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Preboarding Portal** | Send welcome email with portal access before day 1 |
| **Progressive Disclosure** | Reveal tasks in phases (Week 1, Month 1, Quarter 1) |
| **Buddy Assignment** | Auto-assign onboarding buddy from same team |
| **Check-in Cadence** | Scheduled 30/60/90 day check-ins with manager |
| **Feedback Loop** | Onboarding satisfaction survey at 90 days |
